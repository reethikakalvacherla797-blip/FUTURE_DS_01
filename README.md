## 📊TASk-1 Business Sales Performance Dashboard

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Business Sales Performance Dashboard developed using Tableau. The objective was to analyze sales data and extract meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

The dashboard provides a consolidated view of revenue performance, profitability, category contribution, product dominance, and regional sales distribution.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

-Monitor overall revenue and profit performance

-Evaluate profit margin efficiency

-Identify top-performing products

-Analyze category-level contribution

-Compare regional sales distribution

-Track monthly sales trends

---

## 📁 Dataset Used

Superstore Sales Dataset

(Source: Kaggle)

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

-Tableau (Data Visualization & Dashboard Design)

-CSV Dataset

-Business KPI Calculations

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| Metric            | Value          |
| ----------------- | -------------- |
| **Total Revenue** | ₹ 2,297,200.86 |
| **Total Profit**  | ₹ 286,397.02   |
| **Profit Margin** | 12.47%         |


## Calculated Fields Used:

SUM(Sales) → Total Revenue

SUM(Profit) → Total Profit

SUM(Profit) / SUM(Sales) → Profit Margin %

## 📌 Key Insights

1.Technology category contributes the highest share of total revenue.

2.Sales show a strong upward trend in Q4, indicating seasonal demand spikes.

3.The East region generates the highest sales contribution among all regions.

4.Revenue distribution follows the Pareto principle, where a limited number of products drive a significant portion of total sales.

5.The overall profit margin of 12.47% suggests moderate profitability, with improvement opportunities in lower-performing categories such as Furniture.
